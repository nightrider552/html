SMARTER TECHNOLOGY FOR ALL



Lenovo’s story has always been about shaping computing intelligence to create a better world. With the world’s widest portfolio of technology products, we deliver our vision of Smarter Technology for All through products, solutions, software, and services that individuals, communities, businesses, and entire populations need to fulfill their potential. Lenovo is known for its commitment to innovation, sustainability, and delivering “smarter technology for all.” It remains the world’s largest PC vendor by unit sales and continues to expand into cloud, AI, and infrastructure solutions.

Key Higlights
  • Rebranding and global expansion: Legend rebranded as Lenovo in 2003, with the name combining "Le-" from Legend and "-novo" (Latin for "new"). A significant step in its global expansion was the acquisition of IBM's PC business in 2005, which included the ThinkPad line.
  • Product portfolio: Lenovo offers a wide range of products including computers, tablets, smartphones, servers, and related services. Notable brands include ThinkPad, IdeaPad, Yoga, and Legion for laptops, and IdeaCentre and ThinkCentre for desktops.
  • Industry leadership: Since 2013, Lenovo has been the world's largest personal computer vendor by unit sales. As of 2024, it holds about 25.5% of the global PC market share. The company also became the largest provider for TOP500 supercomputers in 2018.

Vision and innovation

 Lenovo aims to provide "smarter technology for all" and contribute to a more inclusive and sustainable world through its offerings. The company invests in research and development, particularly in areas like AI, 5G, and foldable technologies. Lenovo focuses on creating user-centric technology that addresses real-world issues and improves daily life. It incorporates AI features into its products and seeks to be a leader in the AI era.

Global reach and operations

 Lenovo operates in over 180 markets with a strong global supply chain and manufacturing. The company owns most of its manufacturing facilities, allowing for regional customization and control over product development and supply chain. With facilities in various countries, Lenovo's hybrid manufacturing model ensures efficiency, quality, and quick time-to-market.

Sustainibility

Our Path To Net Zero

Climate change affects everyone. At Lenovo, our goal is to provide smarter technology that builds a brighter, more sustainable future for our customers, colleagues, communities, and the planet. In January 2023, we made a commitment to reach net-zero greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions by 2050. Our approach to get there is scientific, collaborative and accountable.
